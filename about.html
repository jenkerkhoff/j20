About Me

Clearing up the disinformation about me.

Ever since I asked to prosecute the J20 protesters, there's been a lot of bad information circulating about me. So, here's my opportunity to clear that all up. I'm going to be brutally honest here, so hold on tight!

  • I'm an Assistant US Attorneya in the District of Columbia, and the deputy chief of the felony division

  • I live in Alexandria, Virginia.

  • I'm married to Nate who just got a promotion - I'm really proud of him.

  • I was born September 2. I won't tell you the year, but not really over the hill. Only kind of over the hill.

  • I have a Virgo tattoo - some people say it looks trashy, but I can't see it because it's on my shoulder.

  • I gave money to Republican candidates.

  • I don't like anarchists. They advocate for a world where everyone is free of leaders. I don't like that. I like our leader Trump.

  • I met Jeff Sessions and he was wonderful. I love working for him. He gives me so many resources to put all these people away.

  • I really like working with John Borchert. He's so smart and eloquent. I wish I could be likeable by a jury.

  • Sometimes I lie about how much work I have to do, but only because I really really want Judge Liebovitz to like me. She's such a powerful woman. One day I'll get to be a judge like her and send even more people to jail.

  • I don't follow up on things Judge Liebovitz tells me to do around Discovery, because I don't really understand. Plus, she helps me out a lot and I really like that.

  • I didn't actually read the DC code. That's why I accidentally charged all those anarchists with the wrong APO charge. But the judge is so kind, she often tries my case for me, so it's okay. Let's keep this as our little secret.

  • My favorite coffee is Compass Coffee at 7th and F St NW. I like to get lattes.

  • I enjoy shopping at the Trader Joes in Clarendon. My favorite thing to buy is those sparkling water drinks.

  • I went to Law School at GW

  • I have 31 purses - 1 for each day of the month. I sort them in my walk-in closet. My favorite one is red, but not because I'm a communist. I like red because it's on the Amercian flag and America is the greatest country on earth.

  • A lot of people think I like wearing heels. I don't. I go to court in flip flops, then change into heels before the judge comes in.

  • My nicknames include: Jen, Jenny, and JennyK

  • I still have a landline: 202-965-1260
